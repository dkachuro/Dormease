1. Subject of the Contract

This contract defines the terms of accommodation for student {{ full_name }} at the Astana IT University dormitory (hereinafter referred to as the “University”).

The University provides the student with a place in the dormitory for the duration of the academic year, with the possibility of extension upon review.

2. Rights and Obligations of the Parties

The University undertakes to:

  • Provide a furnished accommodation place;
  • Ensure proper sanitary and living conditions;
  • Inform the student of the dormitory rules.

The Student undertakes to:

  • Comply with internal rules and regulations of the dormitory;
  • Take good care of property and maintain order;
  • Make timely payments, if applicable;
  • Respect community rules, including quiet hours and cleanliness.

3. Contract Term

This contract enters into force from the date of application submission — {{ submitted_date }} — and is valid until the end of the academic year. Extension is possible by mutual agreement of the parties.

4. Termination of the Contract

The contract may be terminated early in the following cases:

  • Expulsion of the student from the University;
  • Repeated violation of dormitory rules;
  • By mutual consent of both parties.

5. Final Provisions

This contract is made in two copies, one for each party. Both copies have equal legal force.
